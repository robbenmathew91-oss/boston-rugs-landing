Organic and Hand Washed Care

Fine wool and silk rugs contain natural oils and lanolin that protect the fibers. Modern steam cleaning or machine scrubbing strips these natural protectors, causing fibers to dry out, grow brittle, and crack. At Noor, we use a multi-step traditional process: organic hand washing, gentle dust extraction, and flat sun-drying. Our green methods prevent color bleeding and preserve authentic textures.

We handle all classes of heirloom rugs, including Persian, Turkish, Caucasian, Indian, Chinese, and Navajo weaves. We also provide complimentary local pickup and delivery throughout Greater Boston.

  • pH-Balanced Organic Vegetable Soap Baths
  • Natural Sun-Drying to Keep Fibers Soft
  • Mothproofing and Fiber Protection Treatments
