Woven History

The Art of Generational Weaving

Each rug in our showroom is selected for its artistry, density, and historical significance. Hand-knotted node by node in historic weaving centers across Persia, Turkey, and India, our rugs take anywhere from six months to multiple years to complete.

Natural Vegetable Dyes

Rich, living colors extracted from madder root, indigo, pomegranate skins, and walnut husks that age beautifully over decades.

Generational Wool & Silk

Hand-spun Highland wool rich in natural lanolin provides natural stain resistance and a luxurious, soft lustre underfoot.

Showroom Services

Lifetime Rug Stewardship

A premium hand-knotted rug is an investment that outlives generations. We provide expert care to preserve its beauty and value.

Organic Hand Wash

Traditional submersion wash using pH-balanced organic soaps and cold spring water. Preserves natural lanolin and prevents color bleeding.

Master Restoration

Expert re-weaving of worn fringes, damaged selvages, and moth damage. We match original vegetable dye wool fibers perfectly.

In-Home Consultation

Our design curators bring selected rugs to your Boston area home. See the colors in your unique lighting and environment before purchasing.
