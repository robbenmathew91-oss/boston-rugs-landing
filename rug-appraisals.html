Expert and Legally Binding Evaluations

Determining the authentic market value of a hand-knotted Oriental or Persian rug requires extensive scholarship and market experience. Led by Harvard Law alumnus and master weaver Mohammad "Mo" Nooraee, Noor Oriental Rugs, Inc. provides certified, legally binding appraisals accepted by all major insurance companies, estate executors, and the IRS. For charitable donations over $500, we manage IRS Form 8283 completion and recipient signatures.

Our appraisals include exhaustive documentation of origin, age, weaving style, dimensions, materials, and condition metrics, giving you absolute protection and accurate paper trails.
