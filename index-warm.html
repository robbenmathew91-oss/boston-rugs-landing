Woven History

Noor Oriental Rugs: Three Centuries of Weaving Artistry

For over three hundred years, the Nooraee family has transmitted the precious heritage of weaving Persian rugs from generation to generation. Today, founder, Harvard Law alumnus, and president Mo Nooraee continues that legacy as a master weaver and one of the nation's foremost certified appraisers and restorers of fine hand-knotted rugs.

Traditional Vegetable Dyes

We respect the pedigree of hand-made rugs woven from natural fibers, handspun wool, and non-synthetic, color-fast dyes.

Museum-Quality Conservation

Himself a master weaver, Mr. Nooraee ensures that every rug is treated with the highest level of care using time-honored methods.
