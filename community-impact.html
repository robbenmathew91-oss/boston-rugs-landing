Community Impact: Giving Back

Committed to community service in Cambridge & Boston. Facilitating rug donations, shelter support, and seasonal outreach.

Outreach Programs & Sufi Service

Born into a long family heritage of master weavers, Mo Nooraee was taught early on that service is the ultimate treasure. Since 1979, Noor Oriental Rugs, Inc. has been deeply committed to the Cambridge and Boston communities, donating hundreds of thousands of dollars in value of carpets, clothing, and shelter meals. We organize direct donations to verified 501(c)(3) organizations and host weekly outreach initiatives.

Through the Sufi Service Committee (Boston), Mo Nooraee serves as Director to lead local initiatives. Our regular operations include food bank transportation, winter coat drives, and weekly homeless shelter brunches.

  • Partnerships with local 501(c)(3) charities
  • Free collection, wash, and appraisal for donations
  • IRS Tax Form 8283 processing and recipient signatures
  • Weekly shelter brunch sponsorships and clothing drives
