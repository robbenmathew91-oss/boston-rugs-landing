Preserving Weaving Legacy

Fine Persian and Oriental rugs are woven masterpieces designed to endure generations. However, moth damage, worn fringes, and pile wear require traditional care to preserve their value and aesthetic integrity. Led by master weaver Mo Nooraee, we source matching handspun highland wool and vegetable dyes to repair antique rugs invisibly.

At Noor Oriental Rugs, Inc., we reject short-cut machine repairs. Every thread is matched for fiber count, spin density, and color run, ensuring that the structural and historical value of your heirloom rug is preserved completely.

  • Traditional Pile Re-weaving
  • Fringe Reconstruction & Overcasting
  • Moth Damage & Hole Restoration
  • Color Conservation & Dye Matching
